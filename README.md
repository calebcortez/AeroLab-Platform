# AeroLab Platform 1

My individual contributions to a team-designed portable elevated access platform for aircraft and spacecraft mockup maintenance (UH-Clear Lake, SCRCA Conference 2026).

**My work:** Onshape CAD assemblies of the ladder and gate systems, plus structural analysis of the platform posts - bending, axial, and buckling stress with von Mises failure analysis, validated against ANSI/OSHA safety codes.
